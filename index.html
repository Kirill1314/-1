Terraria bosses

The Eye Of Cthulhu

The Eye of Cthulhu (Eye of Cthulhu) is the boss. It is called with the help of a suspicious eye, which can be made from 6 lenses on a demonic altar or a bloody altar, as well as found in a golden chest. You can only summon the Eye of Cthulhu at night. Also, if the player's maximum health is 200 or more health units, the player has more than 10 units of protection and more than 3 NPCs live in the world, there is a chance that he will appear himself, while the player will receive a message: "You feel an evil presence watching you" (You feel someone's evil attention ...). After his murder, a Dryad may come to you

The Devourer of Worlds

The Eater of Worlds is a boss that is summoned either with the help of worm food in the territory of corruption, or after the destruction of three shadow spheres. Demonite ore or shadow scales can fall out of each part of his body, and after the death of the last segment, a large amount of demonite ore and small medicinal potions. There is no other way to get shadow scales.

Skeletron

Skeletron (Skeletron) — a pre-modern boss. In order to find him, you need to find a dungeon. At the very entrance there will be an old man (Old Man) with whom you need to start a conversation. If you started a conversation during the day, the old man will answer: "If you want to enter the dungeon, come at night," and nothing else will happen. But if you start a conversation at night, then you Skeleton that will attack you. After finishing the battle and defeating the boss, the player will be able to enter the dungeon.

Wall_of_Flesh

Wall of Flesh — a boss added in update 1.1. Is the key boss in the game, since after killing him, the game switches to hard mode. The henchmen are the thirsty one and the leech. To summon him, you need to throw a voodoo guide doll into the lava while in hell, while the guide must be alive, otherwise the boss will not come. After the call, the guide dies, but comes the next morning. Read more

Skeletron Prime

Skeletron Prime is a hardcore mechanical boss added in Update 1.1. It is a complicated version of Skeletron. You can summon it with the help of a mechanical skull. It has 4 mechanical limbs and a head. Neither the hands nor the head are subject to dazing and poisoning, and the head separately is also penetrated. When the head starts spinning, spikes come out of it, damage and protection increase. Read more

Twins

Gemini (The Twins) is a mechanical boss, a hardcore version of the Eye of Cthulhu. Caused by a mechanical eye. Since version 1.2, there is a small chance that they themselves will appear after the inscription: "It's going to be a terrible night..."(«This is going to be a terrible night…») Each eye has its own individual health. Red is a Retinizer, shoots purple lasers, and green is a Spasm, shoots a cursed flame. They are connected by a thread of flesh that connects them together. When killing or moving the eyes a long distance from each other, the thread connecting them will not be visible. Read more

The Planter

The planter is called upon after destroying the bud of the Planter with a pickaxe or drill. The Planter's Bud appears in the underground jungle only after all three mechanical bosses have been defeated (Before version 1.2.3, only one mechanical boss had to be killed). Read more

The Golem

The golem is summoned with the help of a lizard battery (they can be found in chests inside the jungle temple or knocked out from the lizards) on the lizard altar in the jungle temple. To gain access to the Lizard Temple, you must kill the Planter and open the lizard temple with the key that fell from it. Read more

The Destroyer

The Destroyer is a mechanical boss added in Update 1.1. It is a hard—mod version of the Devourer of Worlds. It can be called only at night with the help of a mechanical worm. Unlike the Devourer of Worlds, it is called in any biome. In addition to the usual ramming attack, the Destroyer shoots red lasers at the player from each of its segments. Lasers can pass through platforms. Read more

A lunatic sectarian

A lunatic sectarian is summoned when killing 4 sectarians who appear at the entrance to the Dungeon after defeating the Golem. After defeating him, Lunar events begin.Although the Sectarian Lunatic has only one phase, his attacks become stronger with decreasing health. He can fly using his attacks: fireballs, creating an electric sphere that shoots lightning in a circle, creating a giant snowflake that lets ice fragments into the player, shadow lights and shooting 5 destructible spheres. Read more

MoonLord

Moon Lord is the final boss of Terraria in version 1.3.0.5, from which the most powerful items in the game fall out. Defeating him alone can be a very difficult challenge even with the best equipment. He appears after defeating all 4 Heavenly Towers during Lunar Events, or after using the Heavenly Seal. The Moon Lord has 2 arms and a head that should be "killed" by dealing damage to the eyes on them. When all three eyes are destroyed, the core opens — the heart, which also needs to be destroyed. The hands shoot homing projectiles and can summon phantom versions of the True Eye of Cthulhu to throw them at the player. Read more
